Things to know before going to Ajanta Caves

  • The caves are closed on Mondays.

  • Flash photography is not allowed, since the murals and paintings are old, light from the flashes impact them.

  • Tickets to see ajanta caves are priced at 600 rupees by the government official website.

  • Exploring Ajanta caves will be long, takes around 2-3 hours if you want to rush through all the sites fast.

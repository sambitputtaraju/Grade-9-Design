Who are we?

We are the ajanta community, a non profit organization here to provide you information about the history-rich ancient caves, Ajanta caves, through our website.

We were truly fascinated after we had a look at Ajanta Caves, The architecture, senescence , the huge size and the history it had left behind. We thought Ajanta caves is not getting the popularity it deserves, so we have created this non profit organization and website.
